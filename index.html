Room Temperature Water (Serving size: 1)

An essential meal for everyday life.

Instructions
1. Place ice inside a cup.
2. Wait 16-20 minutes for it to melt.
3. Enjoy!

Hard Boiled Eggs

A breakfast packed with protein.

Instructions
1. Aquire chicks.
2. After six weeks, build a coop for your new family, be sure to include a nest box. Feed them daily for about 16-24 weeks.
3. Once fully grown and happy, your chickens will return your kindness in the physical form of eggs. Collect them from nest box.
4. Use the first recipe you learned to boil a pot of water.
5. Once the water has boiled, reduce heat, and drop in a few eggs.
6. Let it cook for 4-5 minutes.
7. Drain, cool in ice water, and peel.

Fennel and Chicken Stew (Serving size: 8)

A yummy stew with no hassle.

Instructions
1. Sauté olive oil, garlic, and onion over medium heat in a large nonstick stockpot or Dutch oven.
2. Add celery and fennel and sauté briefly, then add the tomatoes.
3. Say one last goodbye to your chicken.
4. Add broth, chicken, potatoes, and seasonings. Cook at a simmer over medium heat until the chicken is done and the potatoes are tender, about 20-25 minutes.
